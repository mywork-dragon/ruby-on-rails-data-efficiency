MIGHTYSIGNAL PRIVACY FOR FACEBOOK RECRUITING

Updated: October 2, 2016

This policy is here to help you understand what information we collect at MightySignal when we run advertisements on Facebook for recruiting.

INFORMATION WE COLLECT AND RECEIVE

We collect different kinds of information. Here are the types of information we collect:

A link to your LinkedIn profile. You enter this on the Facebook ad form.

Your email address. You enter this on the Facebook ad form.

Your full name. You enter this on the Facebook ad form.

HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

We use your information only to:

- Look at your background to see if we'd like to speak with you
- Contact you by email
